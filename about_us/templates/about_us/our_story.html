The Inspiration

The name COVE comes from a simple belief: love is the greatest ingredient in everything we do.

We fell in love with the idea of connecting people—not just to products, but to the artisans, the makers, and the families behind them. Every item we curate is chosen with intention. We ask ourselves: Does this bring joy? Does this reflect care? Does it make someone's day a little brighter?
