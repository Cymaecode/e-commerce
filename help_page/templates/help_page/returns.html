Returns & Exchanges

Hassle-free returns within 30 days

Our Return Policy

We want you to love everything you buy from COVE. If something isn't perfect, we're here to help!

30 Days

Return within 30 days of delivery

Unused Condition

Items must be unused with tags attached

Original Packaging

Return in original packaging

How to Start a Return

1

Log Into Your Account

Go to Order History and select the order containing the item you wish to return.

2

Select Items & Reason

Choose the items you want to return and select the reason for return.

3

Print Return Label

Generate and print your prepaid return shipping label.

4

Pack & Ship

Pack your items securely with all original packaging and tags, attach the label, and drop off at any shipping carrier location.

Guest Returns: If you checked out as a guest, please contact our support team at support@cove.com with your order number to initiate a return.

Refund Information

Processing Time

Once we receive your return, please allow 5-7 business days for processing. You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is issued.

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable. Return shipping is free for all domestic returns. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

Exchanges

Want a different size or color? The fastest way to get what you want is to:

Return the item you have (following the steps above)
Place a new order for the desired item

This ensures you get your new item as quickly as possible without waiting for the return to process.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Final sale or clearance items
  • Intimates, lingerie, and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Gift cards
  • Items worn, washed, or with missing tags

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us immediately at:

support@cove.com

Please include your order number and photos of the item, and we'll make it right as quickly as possible.

Still have questions? Check out our FAQs or contact us for assistance.
