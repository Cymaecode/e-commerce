Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by:

Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
Logging into your account and viewing your order history
Using the tracking number on the carrier's website

Please allow 24 hours for tracking information to update after receiving your shipping confirmation.

Shipping FAQs

Do you ship to PO boxes?

Yes, we ship to PO boxes via USPS for standard shipping only. Express and overnight shipping require a physical address.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Please contact us immediately at support@cove.com if you need to change your shipping address. We can only modify addresses before the order is processed for shipping.

What happens if my package is lost or stolen?

If your tracking shows "delivered" but you haven't received your package, please check with neighbors or your local post office. For lost or stolen packages, contact us within 7 days and we'll help file a claim with the carrier.

Do you offer expedited shipping for international orders?

Yes, expedited international shipping options are available at checkout. Rates and delivery times vary by destination.
